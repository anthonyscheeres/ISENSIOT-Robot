Retrieve the estimated altitude from the last read of the B\+M\+E680 device. 

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
this is calcualted from the pressure measurement. It is not reliable for high altitudes, and it is affected by the ambient (barometric) pressure due to weather. For that reason, the B\+M\+E680 is better for measuring changes in altitude versus absolute altitude.
\end{DoxyNote}
